# Titan Residuum

You can only acquire Titan Residuum from:

1. Your weekly Mythic+ chest
2. Scrapping epic Azerite pieces (not blues or greens)
3. Island Expedition weekly that returns Titan Residdum (4,500 to 5,000)
4. Purchasing and scrapping Benthic Azerite pieces

Azerite pieces can be scrapped for Titan Residuum for the following amounts:

| Level | Titan Residuum | Benthic Gear | Residuum per pearl |
| ------|----------------|--------------|--------------------|
| 340   | 1 |  |  |
| 355   | 3 |  |  |
| 370   | 12 |  |  |
| 385   | 35 | 5 pearls | 1.42 |
| 400   | 200 | 5 + 20 | 4.60 |
| 415   | 400 | 5 + 20 + 50 | 4.87 |
| 430   | 2000  | 5 + 20 + 50 + 350 | 4.0  |
| 445   | 10000  |  |   |

You can then purchase Azerite pieces

| Level        | Titan Residuum |
|--------------|----------------|
| Random 415   | 1,725          |
| Random 430   | 9,000          |
| Random 445   | 47,500         |
| Specific 445 | 200,000        |

# Loot

* Weekly Chest contains 1 item
* Azerite pieces cannot be obtained from Mythic+ except by purchasing with Titan Residuum
* You can now bonus roll for loot when completing a Mythic+
* Loot can Warforge up to +10, and Titanforge from +15 up to a total maximum item level of +425

# Combat Changes

* No gear or talent changes allowed in Mythic+ instances (you must zone out, change gear and/or talents, then zone back in)

## Combat Rez

* Dungeon starts with 1 shared cooldown combat rez
* A new combat rez charge is added every 10 mins
* Combat rez charges max out at 5

## Reaping (Season 2)

* Risen Soul melees to apply Reap Soul (Hits for 2% health every 3 secs, stacking)
* Tormented Soul casts Grave Bolt, 5 sec interruptible cast that hits for 25% health
* Lost Soul casts frontal cone Shadow Smash, reducing health by 20% for 1 minute (stacking)
* Lost Soul casts Expel Soul on dying, damages and knocks back, reducing healing by 10% for 30 secs, stacking

## Beguiling (Season 3)

### Enchanted Emissary

* Has a big bubble around them
* Their aura reflects damage to the pack (150%)
* Damage knocks them back, so get a DPS to knock them away from the rest of the pack, and kill the pack
* It effectively can't be killed but will teleport away after 20 secs

### Void-touched Emissary

* Purple / shadow looking mob
* Has True Sight, so can't be skipped
* Queen's Decree: Hide is a 9 second cast does a 50% health damage nuke to the group, but can be line of sighted
* If you get hit by the cast, it stacks 50% more damage on the next hit (and so on)
* If you can, burn it down before it gets two casts, use immunities / resets if you wish

### Emissary of the Tides

* Watery looking mob
* Grants an Unstoppable aura to the pack which makes them immune to all CC, interrupts, stuns, knockbacks etc
* CC the emissary (CC lasts for only 8 secs but has no diminishing returns) and kill the pack, then kill the emissary
